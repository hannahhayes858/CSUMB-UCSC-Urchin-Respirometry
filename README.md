## What is the thermal critical minimum, maximum and optimal temperature for purplee sea urchins (Strongylocentrotus purpuratus)?

### Background
As sea temperatures continue to rise, many ecosytems have been pushed into alternative states due to changes in species interactions. A well documented case of this is along the 
central and northern California coast. During the marine heat wave from 2014-2014, there was about a 90% loss of kelp forests due to a major shift in pruple sea urchin grazing 
behavior. Ecophysiology digs into the mechanistic reasoning of why these behavior shifts occur. For our project we are interested in understand what urchins thermal optima, minimums 
and maximums lie on a temperature curve. We hope to use this thermal performance curve to compare with other species they interact with such as kelp, lobsters, and CA sheephead.
